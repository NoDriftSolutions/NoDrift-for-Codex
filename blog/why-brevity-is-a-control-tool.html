Why Brevity Is A Control Tool

A short answer is not automatically better, but a bounded answer is easier to inspect, correct, and approve.

NoDrift conceptBrevity check
User problemThe answer becomes too long to control.
Reading depthPractical

The problem

Long answers can hide drift. They can bury the actual result under background, explanation, alternatives, and confident extra framing the user did not request.

What brevity protects

Brevity protects attention. It makes it easier to see whether the AI agent answered the question, named the source, stayed inside approval, and stopped at the boundary.

How NoDrift helps

NoDrift encourages answer shapes that match the task. A status check should not become an essay. A yes-or-no question should not become a product strategy.

Practical hint

Ask for the smallest useful answer: "Answer in four lines: result, source, risk, next approval." That is not less serious. It is more controlled.
