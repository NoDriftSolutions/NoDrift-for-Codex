Carryover and continuity

How To Stop A Conversation From Becoming The Project

The conversation is not the project. It is one stream of LLM-connected work that has to stay tied to records, decisions, files, and current state.

NoDrift conceptConversation-to-project boundary
User problemThe chat becomes the authority instead of the project record.
Reading depthPractical

The problem

Long agentic AI conversations can start to feel like the whole project. That is risky. A conversation can forget, compress, infer, or blend old and new context.

The project needs records

Important work needs current project state, source truth, decisions, open questions, approvals, corrections, and handoff notes. Those records keep the AI agent from depending only on chat flow.

NoDrift's role

NoDrift keeps the AI agent tied to the user-side project workspace. received model information does not become true just because it appears in the conversation.

Practical hint

When a conversation starts carrying too much authority, stop and ask: "Where is this recorded outside the chat?" If there is no record, treat it as unconfirmed until preserved or checked.
