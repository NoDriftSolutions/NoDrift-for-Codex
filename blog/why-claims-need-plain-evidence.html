Why Claims Need Plain Evidence

A claim is only useful when the user can see what supports it.

NoDrift conceptClaim discipline
User problemThe AI agent makes statements stronger than the evidence.
Reading depthFoundation

The problem

LLM-generated language can make a weak claim sound settled. A polished statement may hide that the source was not checked.

What plain evidence does

Plain evidence reduces interpretation. It names the file, page, record, approved wording, or observed fact that supports the claim.

How NoDrift helps

NoDrift pushes the AI agent to separate claim, evidence, and inference. That lets the user decide whether the claim is strong enough to use.

Practical hint

Ask: "Is this verified, inferred, or proposed?" The answer should not blur those three states.
