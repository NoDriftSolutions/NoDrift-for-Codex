Why Interpretation Is A Control Problem

The risk is not only what the LLM generated. The risk is also how the AI agent interprets that information after it enters the workspace.

NoDrift conceptInterpretation control
User problemThe same model information is treated as meaning different things.
Reading depthFoundation

Meaning is a control point

received model information can be interpreted as advice, proof, permission, a plan, a correction, or an instruction. Those are not the same. If the wrong meaning is accepted by the AI agent, the next move can be wrong even when the answer sounded useful.

Example

The LLM says, "This looks ready." That could mean the draft reads smoothly. It could also be mistaken as a readiness claim. Without interpretation control, the phrase may become stronger than the evidence supports.

NoDrift's role

NoDrift gives the AI agent reception-side controls for asking clear questions: What is being claimed? What is the source? What is approved? What is still only a draft? What should not move forward yet?

Practical hint

Before acting around received model information, name what kind of communication it is. A proposal should not be handled like a decision. A confident sentence should not be handled like proof.
